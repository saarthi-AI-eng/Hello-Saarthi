11. HARDWARE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS (Development):
• Apple M2/M3 Mac with 32GB RAM, or
• Linux machine with NVIDIA GPU (16GB+ VRAM)
• 500GB SSD storage

RECOMMENDED (Production PoC):
• Apple M3 Max with 64GB RAM, or
• Linux server: NVIDIA A100 40GB or 2x RTX 4090
• 1TB SSD storage

ESTIMATED COSTS:
• Local deployment: $0 ongoing (one-time hardware)
• If cloud needed: ~$500-1000/month for GPU instances

12. RISK MITIGATION
================================================================================

RISK 1: OCR accuracy on complex handwritten formulas
MITIGATION: LLM post-processing for error correction, manual QA on 20% sample

RISK 2: Model too slow on local hardware
MITIGATION: Use quantized models, implement caching for common queries

RISK 3: Retrieval misses relevant content
MITIGATION: Hybrid retrieval (ColBERT + Dense + BM25), extensive testing

RISK 4: Knowledge graph construction too time-consuming
MITIGATION: Start with simple Topic → Content links, expand incrementally

RISK 5: Student confusion from incorrect math solutions
MITIGATION: Confidence scoring, "Check this answer" disclaimers, human review
